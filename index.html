Short Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), working with Prof. Sen Li. I completed my Ph.D. in Civil Engineering at HKUST in 2025, and obtained my B.Eng. in Automation from Tongji University in 2020. I was a visiting student at University of California, Berkeley, hosted by Prof. Kameshwar Poolla.

Research Interests

  • Theory: network modeling, optimization, optimal control, game theory

  • Application: transportation electrification, emerging mobility, urban logistics, coupled power-transportation systems
